Blue Flamingo, LLC is a business I started at the beginning of 2021. A few months prior, I left my steady and well-paid office job in pursuit for something more challenging. I wanted to start my own business - some type of service that would provide value to different people I knew and have worked with before.

I settled on an online invoicing service that was geared towards small business owners. My parents own a small business, their friends own small businesses, and all of the contractors I have ever hired were all small, locally owned businesses.

These are all smart and hard working people that are skilled at their trades. Most of them operated primary by check and cash payments. I wanted to provide them a service to make it easier to send and collect their invoices, that way they can stop chasing payments and get back to what they do best: continuing to make their business great.
